Co-President

Degree: Bachelors of Commerce

Faculty: Asper School of Business
Major(s): Management Information Systems & Supply Chain Management

I joined MISA as a member because I was very fond of the MIS major while taking the higher-level classes in Fall 2017. I was so fond of the classes that I decided to switch career paths into MIS from Accounting. After completing all of the MIS classes offered at the Asper School of Business, I was fortunate to have the opportunity to apply my knowledge in a real-world setting. My one goal is to bring MISA to places that the University of Manitoba hasn't seen. If you see me in the hallways at school (you will), be sure to stop me and say hi! I can answer any questions you have or we can strike up a conversation about whatever you want.
