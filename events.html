Thurs, Sept 19

Introduction to
Innovation Major

5:30 - 7:00pm
Drake Rm. 103

Introduction to Innovation Major is our first educational event where we invite industry professionals to speak about their journey of taking either Management Information Systems (MIS) as a major from the University of Manitoba to their future career or landing into a MIS job through other means. This event will consist of a Q&A session. MISA team will be providing free food for this event.

FRI, Sept 20

Technology Clubs
Mixer

4:30 - 6:00pm
The Hub

Technology Clubs Mixer is a gathering where MISA members will mix with other technology groups at the University of Manitoba’s Fort Garry campus such as Computer Science Students’ Association (CSSA), DevClub, and WICS (Women in Computer Science). MISA members will have the opportunity to get to know students from other groups who share the same passion and interest or even similar career goals.

What is a Hackathon
Competition?

5:30 - 7:00 pm
Drake Rm. 103

Hackathon is a competition where students with different expertise and backgrounds come together to develop a solution for real-world issues. The organizers of the event will be discussing more in-depth detail of what Hackathon is and the benefits of joining this competition. This event will also consist of a Q&A session so that you will have a full understanding of the Hackathon Competition.
