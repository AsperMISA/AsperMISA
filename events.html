Thurs, Sept 19

Introduction to Innovation Major

5:30 - 7:00pm
Drake Rm. 103

Introduction to Innovation Major is our first educational event where we invite a MIS professor and professionals to speak about their journey of taking either Management Information Systems (MIS) as a major from the University of Manitoba to their future career or landing into a MIS job through other means. This event will consist of a Q&A session. MISA team will be providing free food for this event.

Technology Clubs Mixer

4:30 - 6:00pm
SSA Lounge

Technology Clubs Mixer is a gathering where MISA members will mix with other technology groups at the University of Manitoba’s Fort Garry campus such as Computer Science Students’ Association (CSSA), .devClub, and WICS (Women in Computer Science). MISA members will have the opportunity to get to know students from other groups who share the same passion and interest or even similar career goals.
