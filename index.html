About MISA

Management Information Systems Association (MISA) is an Innovation-focused student group within the I.H. Asper School of Business at the University of Manitoba.

Our mission is to provide a service to the University of Manitoba community regarding further education of MIS (e.g., what the MIS major is, how MIS is used in professional careers, and how businesses use technology to solve business problems). To accomplish this mission, MISA hosts multiple educational events with working professionals and hosts multiple networking events to connect students with today's business leaders.

What is MIS?

MIS is innovation - improving businesses and technologies. Today's market leaders in every industry uses MIS to gain a competitive advantage over their competitors. It is one of the leading fields in the business world due to its multi-disciplinary approach of incorporating elements of business, technology, and engineering.
