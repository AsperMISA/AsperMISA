About MISA

Management Information Systems Association (MISA) is an Innovation-focused student group within the I.H. Asper School of Business at the University of Manitoba

MISA’s main demographic are the Management Information Systems (MIS) students. However, ALL individuals inside and outside the Asper School of Business are welcomed to join the group & attend our events! Membership is FREE for everybody!

What is MIS?

MIS is innovation - changing something for the better. Today's market leaders in every industry uses MIS to gain a competitive advantage over their competitors. It is one of the leading fields in the business world due to its multi-disciplinary approach of incorporating elements of business, technology, and engineering.
